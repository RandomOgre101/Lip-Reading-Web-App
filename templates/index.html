How does it Work?

We are thrilled to present our project solution - a powerful AI model designed to bridge communication gaps for students and everyone with hearing impairments. Let's dive into the intricate workings of this transformative technology.

  • Convolutional Layers: Unveiling Visual Patterns
  • Temporal Pooling: Extracting Essential Insights
  • Bidirectional LSTM: Deciphering the Language
  • Dropout for Robust Learning

Our model starts with Conv3D layers capturing visual cues in lip movements.Temporal MaxPooling follows to understand evolving patterns. Bidirectional LSTM decodes lip movements to language, considering context. Dropout prevents overfitting. Careful initialization and weight loading ensure accuracy and reliability.

A Leap Towards Inclusive Learning: Business and Social Impact

The culmination of our model's architecture brings us one step closer to achieving inclusive education for all students. By recognizing and transcribing lip movements into spoken language, we empower students with hearing impairments to engage with classroom content independently.
